PANIPURI RECIPE

know Your panipuri ?

Ingredient


30-40 Puris (panipuri shells)
1 cup Boiled and mashed potatoes
1 cup Boiled and sprouted moong
1/2 cup Chopped onions
1/2 cup Chopped tomatoes
1/4 cup Chopped coriander leaves
1/4 cup Tamarind chutney
1/4 cup Mint-coriander chutney
1 teaspoon Chaat masala
1 teaspoon Roasted cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon Red chili powder
Salt to taste & Water as required
steps to make?

Instruction

1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, boiled sprouted moong, chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, and chopped coriander leaves.

2. Add chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt to the mixture. Mix well.

3. Make a small hole in each puri and fill it with the prepared mixture.

4. Add tamarind chutney and mint-coriander chutney to each filled puri.

5. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Panipuri!
